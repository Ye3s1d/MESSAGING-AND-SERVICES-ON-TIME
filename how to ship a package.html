Get Your Package Ready

First things first - you need to pack your items in a sturdy box. If you don’t have one, you can order supplies online or buy.
• Seal your box with plastic or nylon tape at least two inches wide. Don’t use duct tape.
• Wrap items separately and use cushioning material.
• Keep in mind, there are additional rules for special-care and prohibited items.
• You’ll need to know the dimensions and weight of your box.




• Get a Label and Choose how fast you want your package to arrive.




We’ll Do the Rest

Almost done! You’ve got three options to get it to us:
• We can come to you Schedule a pickup.
• Drop it off at a us location. Be sure to bring your identification.
