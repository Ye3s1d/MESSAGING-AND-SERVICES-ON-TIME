About Us

MESSAGING AND SERVICES ON TIME is a new company in logistics and communication services in the SANTANDER department, with the objective of providing solutions in shipping and handling of documents and parcels to individuals and small, medium and large companies.



Mission




Provide a timely, specialized, modern and professional service for the full and effective satisfaction of the logistical needs and requirements of our clients.



Vision




As a company to be a leader in the courier and parcel service, a leader in logistics and communication services, efficient and with the best local and national coverage through a timely, reliable service provision and provide some of the best alternatives in the market.



Quality Policy




Achieve the permanent satisfaction of the Clients needs and expectations, with competitiveness, productivity and growth goals. For this purpose, quality standards are ensured in the logistics and communication processes, with a committed and suitable human talent.




VALUES OF OUR COMPANY




• COMMITMENT: Work as a team every day to give our colleagues and clients the best of ourselves.
• EFFICIENCY: With discipline we manage resources to achieve results optimal, we continually seek competitive and sustainable advantages.
• INTEGRITY: We act clearly and consistently, with ethical principles.
• LOYALTY: Be true to ourselves, our values and principles.




WHY CHOOSE US?




• Fast, close and professional customer service.
• We generate continuous innovation and expansion strategies towards the national market.
• We have intelligent systems for tracking and tracing packages and shipments.
• we offer very good and completely transparent courier rates.
• We guarantee fast and safe deliveries.
